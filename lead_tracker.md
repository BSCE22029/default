### Step 2 — Dedicated Cold Email Tool (NOT personal Gmail)
- **Instantly.ai** — best for high-volume cold outreach, domain warmup built-in
- **Lemlist** — best for personalized campaigns with images/video
- **Mailshake** — solid for agencies, good CRM integrations
- **Smartlead.ai** — multi-inbox rotation, high deliverability

### Step 3 — Domain Setup for Cold Outreach
1. Buy a secondary domain (e.g., moizservices.com, moiztech.io)
2. Set up Google Workspace on it
3. Warm up the domain for 3–4 weeks (Instantly/Lemlist do this automatically)
4. Send cold emails FROM the warmed domain, not personal Gmail
